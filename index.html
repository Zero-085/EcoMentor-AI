Explain My Score (Mathematical Formula Breakdown)

Your score represents your annual greenhouse gas emissions expressed in Metric Tonnes of Carbon Dioxide Equivalent (t CO₂e / year). The formulas apply recognized standard global coefficients:

  • Transportation Footprint:
    Daily Distance × 365 days × Vehicle Factor / 1000
    --
  • Dietary Footprint:
    Diet Factor × 365 days / 1000
    --
  • Electricity Footprint:
    Monthly kWh × 12 months × Grid Factor (0.5 kg CO₂/kWh) / 1000
    --
  • Water Footprint:
    Annual utility Tier Factor / 1000
    --

Rating Criteria: Excellent represents footprints below 2.0 tonnes (global Paris target); Good spans 2.0 to 4.5 tonnes; Average covers 4.5 to 8.0 tonnes; Needs Improvement is assigned to any annual footprint exceeding 8.0 tonnes.
